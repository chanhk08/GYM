What BMI is?

Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women. View the BMI tables or use the tool below to compute yours.

Why does BMI matter?

People who are classed as clinically obese possess a greater risk of developing diabetes, as well as cardiovascular diseases, cancer and suffering a stroke. You can use the BMI tool to tell how healthy your weight is for your height.
